Nathalie Dumornay's Biography

Nathalie is a senior studying Congitive and Brain Science at Tufts University. Her aspirations are to conduct research in the field of cognitive science focusing mainly on developmental psychopathology and the way that mental health issues in children and adolescents can be mitigated using cognitive behavioral techniques. Nathalie spent her junior year abroad studying Psychology at University College London. In her free time she enjoys singing and playing guitar.
